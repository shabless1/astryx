## What is Astryx?

Astryx is a **personalized calibration system**. It reads your birth chart — the exact sky at the moment and place you were born — and uses it as a map of your energy, then builds you a multi-sensory **recalibration protocol** across five senses: **Sound, Scent, Taste, Body, and Sight**.

It is **calibration, not prediction.** Astryx doesn't tell your fortune. It reads the patterns in your chart and what you're feeling right now, and suggests gentle, balancing things you can *do* today. Same birth data always produces the same reading — it's a system, not a guess.

> Astryx is a reference and wellness tool. It does **not** diagnose, treat, or cure anything, and it is **not** medical advice. Everything is phrased as "may suggest" or "may correlate with." For health concerns, see a licensed practitioner.

---

## What it can do

- **Generate your Cosmic Blueprint** from your birth date, time, and place.
- **Read what you're feeling today** and translate it into a plain-language diagnostic — which planetary energy may be running high, low, or stuck.
- **Prescribe a 5-sense protocol** tuned to bring you back toward balance: a sound/frequency, a breath, a scent, a tea/taste, and a color.
- **Show your Cosmic Weather** — the day's planetary transits and what they may stir.
- **Run a guided Chamber session** — a tuning-fork session set to music keyed to your chart, with breath guidance and a closing check-in.
- **Let you choose your own music** per planetary tone, save favorites, and build your own custom session.
- **(Practitioner tier)** Run sessions for clients, keep notes, and print a protocol sheet.

## Getting started

1. Open **https://n-pi-jet.vercel.app**.
2. You can use Astryx **without an account** (your reading just won't be saved). Sign in only if you want history saved.
3. Have your **birth details** ready: date, *time* if you know it (more precise — but "I don't know" works fine), and birth **city**.

## How to use it — step by step

### 1 · Intake (your birth blueprint)
- Enter your **name, birth date, birth time, and birth city.**
  - Don't know your birth time? Tap **"I don't know"** — Astryx switches to **Solar Chart** mode. Your planetary *sign* insights stay accurate, but houses, Ascendant, Midheaven, and timing-sensitive details are less precise without a birth time (Astryx labels this clearly).
- **Resonance Scan:** for each of the 10 planets, tap any statement that feels true *right now.* You don't have to answer all of them — only what resonates.
  - Each planet also has a **"This feels balanced & strong for me"** option. Tap it if that area of life feels resourced. (It's the opposite of an issue — it tells Astryx which strengths it can draw from.)
- **In your own words:** optionally describe what's going on. The more honest, the more precise your reading.
- **Set your intention:** what you want to call in.
- Tap **Generate my protocol.**

### 2 · Your Reading (Results)
A single scroll, top to bottom:
- **Your Calibration Today** — the headline: what your body may be asking for, and the five plain things to try today. Tap **"Read your reading ↓"** to move down through the full report.
- **Your Calibration Insight** — the dominant signal in your chart today, in plain language.
- **Today's Cosmic Weather** — the active transits. Open any one to read its effect and a simple suggestion (e.g. *"strike your Mars fork on your own for ~5 minutes"*). *(No audio plays here — sound lives only in the Chamber.)*
- **Your 5-Sense Calibration Plan** — the full Sound / Scent / Taste / Body / Sight protocol cards.
- **Prepare your Chamber** — optional things to gather (brew the tea, ready a fork, set your space). None are required.
- **Your Chamber** — the entry to your session, at the **end** of the report so you pass through your reading first.

### 3 · The Chamber (your session)
This is the heart of Astryx — a guided **planetary tuning-fork session** set to music composed in your chart's frequencies.

- **When you enter, it is silent.** Press the **▶ Play** button in the music panel to begin. *(Nothing plays on its own.)*
- The session moves **fork to fork** — an opening/grounding tone, supporting passes, your main tones held longest, and a close. You can follow along, or strike your own physical forks if you own them. You don't need physical forks — the music carries the frequency.
- **Player controls** (always visible): **▶ Play / ❚❚ Pause · ■ Stop · 🔊 Volume + Mute · a scrubber to seek · ◄◄ / ►► to change songs · ↺ Replay.**
- **Choose your music — Default or Customize:**
  - **Default** — the rite picks the song for each planetary tone automatically.
  - **Customize** — tap **Customize**, then pick from the **list of songs** shown for the tone that's playing (● marks the default, ♫ marks what's playing). Many tones have several songs to choose from.
  - Your choices are **saved across sessions** — the chamber stays the one *you* shaped. **↺ Reset all** returns to defaults anytime.
- **Breath guidance** runs throughout, paced to your element.
- **Close + check-in:** at the end, rate how you feel. (Practitioners also get vagal-tone rating, notes, and a printable protocol sheet.)
- Tap **Exit Session** anytime.

### 4 · Music Library (the **Music** tab)
A space to explore and shape the sound outside a session:
- **Browse** — every song, organized by planet and state. Play any, ♡ favorite it, or ＋ add it to a sequence.
- **Favorites** — your saved songs, play anytime.
- **My Chambers** — **build your own chamber:** assemble a custom playlist of songs, name it, and play it on demand.

### Navigation
The top bar holds: **Home · Intake · Results · Chart · Body Grid · Music · History · Settings.** (Some items appear only after you've generated a reading; Practitioner tools appear on the Practitioner tier.)

## Meet Astryx, your in-app guide

Astryx includes an intelligent guide (also named **Astryx**) you can tap to ask questions — "what does this mean?", "why this tea?", "how do I prepare for my Chamber?"

- The guide can **explain your calibration, prepare you for your Chamber, and clarify** why certain sound, scent, taste, body, color, and breath recommendations appear.
- **The guide does not replace the engine.** Astryx's deterministic engines generate your protocol; the guide only *explains* it. It speaks only from your structured calibration data.
- The guide will **not** invent remedies, change your plan, or make medical claims. For health concerns it points you to a licensed practitioner.

> In short: **the engine generates the protocol; the guide explains the protocol.**
