## 1. What Astryx Is (in plain language)

Astryx is a **multi-sensory calibration system**. You give it your birth details and how you're feeling right now, and it builds you a personalized **Chamber** — a guided session that calibrates five of your senses at once:

- **Sound** you listen to (real-instrument music layered over exact planetary frequencies)
- **Scent** you breathe (essential-oil blend)
- **Taste** you sip (herbal tea blend)
- **Body** you move (breath pattern, posture, touch, movement)
- **Sight** you see (color and sacred-geometry visuals)

All five are generated from the **same blueprint**, so they work together as one calibration rather than five separate things.

Astryx is **not** a horoscope app, a meditation timer, or a music player. It uses real astronomical calculation (the actual positions of the planets at your birth, via NASA-JPL data) as a structured way to **recognize patterns** in you — and then it builds a session designed to bring those patterns back into balance.

**One important promise:** the same birth data and the same symptoms always produce the **exact same session**. Astryx is repeatable and consistent, like a reference instrument — not a random generator.

## 2. The Core Idea: Planet ≠ Remedy

This is the heart of Astryx, and it's what makes it different from every "your sign means X" app.

> **The detected planet identifies the pattern. The state of that planet determines the remedy.**

Astryx looks at two things:
1. **What's active in your chart** (the *pattern* — e.g. Mars, the planet of drive, heat, and action).
2. **How you say you're feeling** (the *state* — e.g. anger, inflammation, flare-ups).

Then it prescribes what **balances** the pattern — never more of it.

- If you report **Mars-excess** symptoms (anger, heat, inflammation), Astryx builds a **cooling** chamber: calming Moon character, blue-green colors, peppermint and chamomile, a slow 4-7-8 exhale breath. It does **not** give you more fire.
- If you report **low drive, coldness, fatigue** (Mars *deficiency*), it builds a **warming, activating** chamber instead.
- This logic runs for **all ten planets** and every state — thirty distinct corrective directions in total.

In short: **Astryx identifies what's out of balance and gives you what would settle it.** That's the whole philosophy.

## 6. Step-by-Step: Your Intake (4 steps)

When you start, you'll enter your **Birth Blueprint** and how you're feeling. There are four short steps.

**Step 0 — Personal**
- Choose **Individual** or **Practitioner**.
- Enter your **full name**, **date of birth**, and **time of birth**.
- Don't know your birth time? Tap **"I don't know."** Astryx switches to **Solar Chart mode** (a standard professional technique) — your reading stays accurate; only house-based detail is approximate, and it's clearly labeled "☉ Solar Chart" everywhere.
- Type your **birth city** and pick it from the dropdown (this fixes your exact coordinates and timezone).

**Step 1 — Resonance Scan**
- You'll see **ten planet cards**, each with a few plain-language statements (e.g. Mars: *"I'm carrying anger, frustration, or inflammation in my body"*).
- **Tap every statement that feels true right now.** This is how Astryx learns your current *state* — it's what drives the corrective direction. Tap as many or as few as fit.

**Step 2 — In Your Own Words** *(optional)*
- A free-text box to describe what's going on. The more honest and specific, the more precise your calibration. You can skip it.

**Step 3 — Set Your Intention**
- Type an intention and/or tap from quick chips (Clarity, Peace, Energy, Grounding, etc.).
- Review the **Calibration Summary** (your chart, activated planets, intention).
- Tap **Generate My Protocol.**

A brief sacred-geometry loader runs while Astryx calculates your chart and builds your protocol.

Yes. Tap "I don't know" for Solar Chart mode. Your reading stays accurate; it's clearly labeled.

No. Same birth data + same symptoms = the same session, always. Only when your *symptoms* change does the corrective direction shift.

Because you reported Mars-*excess* symptoms. Astryx balances the pattern rather than amplifying it — that's the Planet ≠ Remedy principle working as designed.

**Do I need the physical forks/teas?**
No — the digital chamber is complete on its own. The physical products deepen the practice when you want them.

**Is the music random?**
No. The real-instrument track is chosen deterministically from your chart and corrective state (same input → same track, always), and it plays over a frequency synthesis built from your planetary Hz, evolving through five phases.
