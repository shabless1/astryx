Quick Start

From the box to your first session

Six steps, about five minutes. Have your forks nearby.

Activate your access

Go to myastryx.com and tap Sign Up. Use the same email you used to buy your forks — that is what unlocks your full beta access automatically. You can sign up with Google or with an email and password.

Agree to the terms

A one-time screen — “Before you begin” — confirms you’re 18+ and understand Astryx is an observational reference tool, not medical advice. Tick the box and continue.

Build your Birth Blueprint

Enter your name, birth date, birth time, and birth city. Don’t know your exact time? Tap I don’t know and Astryx switches to Solar Chart mode. Choose your body map, run the quick resonance scan (tap what feels true right now), rate your energy, add a few words and an intention — then tap Generate my protocol.

Read Today’s Reading

Astryx shows your carrier planet for the day, why that signal is present, and your fork sequence — the order to work your tones.

Enter the Resonance Chamber

Tap Enter Resonance Chamber, then press ▶ in the music player — that one tap starts the whole guided session. Follow each phase: strike your fork when it says Strike now, hold while it says Hold, and ease off at Release. There’s no clock to watch — just follow the guidance.

Complete & check in

On the last phase, tap Complete Session ✓ and note how you feel. That’s a full calibration. Come back each day for a fresh one tuned to the live sky.

The one rule that matters

Sign up with your fork-order email. If you use a different address, the system won’t recognize your purchase — but you can always reach out and we’ll link it.

02 · Your Birth Blueprint

The one-time resonance scan

A four-part intake that builds your natal chart and your first calibration. You only do the full scan once — after that, a light daily check-in takes seconds.

Personal
Name, birth date, birth time, and birth city (with autocomplete). Choose the body map Astryx uses to guide fork placement — Female, Male, or Prefer not to say.
No birth time?
Tap “I don’t know” to switch to ☉ Solar Chart mode. Sign-based insights stay useful; house, Ascendant, and timing details are flagged as less precise. You can add your time later.
Resonance
Rate your energy right now (Depleted → Charged), note where you’re holding it, then tap the statements that feel true across ten planetary systems — or mark a system “balanced & strong.” The fuller the scan, the more precise your calibration.
Narrative
In your own words, what’s going on right now. Astryx reads it for themes — mental acceleration, emotional heaviness, grounding need, depletion. Optional, but it sharpens the result.
Intention
What you want to call in. Type it, or tap a chip — Clarity, Peace, Energy, Healing, Strength, Grounding, and more. Then Generate my protocol.

A brief animated analysis follows, then your reading appears. From here on, Astryx recomputes a fresh calibration each new day from your saved chart, the live sky, and your daily check-in — it never just replays yesterday.

04 · The three sessions

Which session, when

Today’s Calibration  ·  your daily work

Tuned to your chart and today’s sky. Astryx selects the exact forks and phases your reading calls for. Where a signal is running hot or blocked, it’s gently balanced with its regulating tone rather than amplified — so a session always settles you, never over-stimulates. Same chart, same sky, same session, every time.

Full Body Recalibration  ·  ~35 min

The complete anatomical ladder — an opening ground, then all twelve forks climbing feet to crown, a pause of three slow breaths at the top, and the same ladder swept back down to an Earth close. The same map for everyone; no reading needed. A full-coverage reset.

Chakra Recalibration  ·  ~27 min

The seven centers, crown to root and back, sealed with an Earth grounding. Choose your instrument on the tile: the Solfeggio set (396–963 Hz) or the Planetary forks. The chamber carries any tone you don’t hold in hand, so no center is ever skipped.

Prefer to skip the chooser?

Set a default in Settings → Session Mode (Ask each time / Calibrated / Full Body / Chakra), or just tap a dashboard tile — tiles always launch directly.

05 · Inside the Chamber

Running a session

The Resonance Chamber is a guided, full-screen experience. You follow phases — you don’t operate a wizard, and there’s no timer to watch.

The one gesture that starts everything

Press ▶ in the Chamber Music player. Nothing plays until you do — that single tap begins the music, the pacing, and the phase-by-phase flow together.

Following the cues

Each phase tells you exactly what to do with your fork through a short cue:

Strike now — sound your fork Hold — keep it in place Release… next phase — ease off Ground with the Earth tone Rest in the silence

A HOLD badge shows how long the current phase runs. To linger on a phase, tap ← / → or drag the phase slider; tap ▸ Resume the flow to rejoin the timeline.

What’s on screen

Body map
Where to hold the fork for this phase, with a Front / Back toggle and a “How to use this fork” walkthrough.
Visual modes
Switch the atmosphere without touching the audio: Body (☥, shows the guidance card), Color (◓, a color-therapy field), Mandala (✦, a cymatic pattern).
Chamber Music
Play/pause, previous/next song, replay, stop, volume, and a Default / Customize song picker. Each fork’s frequency lives in the music’s key.
Breath guide
A breathing pattern chosen for your reading (e.g. 4-7-8 or Box Breath) runs quietly throughout, with an animated pacer on breath phases.
Ask Astryx
The ✦ Ask Astryx button is right there if you have a question mid-session.
Finishing

On the last phase tap Complete Session ✓ to save it and check in on how you feel. Exit Session leaves without saving — and if you leave partway, the dashboard offers to resume.

06 · Your Sacred Tones forks

Working your physical forks

Astryx names which fork to strike, shows its frequency, and maps where to hold it. Your set determines how you apply it.

Steel · weighted

On-body. Strike, then rest the stem of the fork against the body point with steady, comfortable contact.

Aluminum · field

Off-body. Strike and hold the fork 4–6 inches from the body — by the ear, or sweeping over the point. Light contact only.

How to strike

  1. Settle with one slow breath.
  2. Strike on a soft surface (your knee or palm) — never metal on metal.
  3. Apply per your set — stem to the point (steel) or hovering near the ear / over the point (aluminum).
  4. Two applications at most; let the tone fully fade.
  5. Close with a sealing breath.

⚠ A few safety notes

Mars is activating — Astryx never places it at the end of a session. Pluto is the most powerful and is always followed by grounding. Intimate or pelvic regions are guided as an off-body sweep (no contact). And if a crystal appears in your plan, Malachite always carries a warning — use polished and sealed pieces only, never raw, never in elixirs.

07 · The 5-Sense Plan

Your Calibration Plan, across five channels

Every reading produces one protocol keyed to a single planet, expressed through five senses you can actually act on.

♪ Sound

The fork and frequency to play, and where on the body to apply it.

◎ Scent

Essential oils and how to use them — diffuse or inhale, a few minutes.

◇ Taste

A tea blend and its botanicals, with a simple brew-and-sip.

⬡ Body

A breath pattern, a posture or placement, and a small movement.

✦ Sight

A color to soften your vision into — and sometimes a sacred-geometry form.

An integration note ties them into one flow: begin with sound, layer scent, sip the tea, breathe, hold placement, soften into the color — about 20–30 minutes. Any safety notes from the botanicals, minerals, or crystals in your plan are always shown.

08 · Ask Astryx

Your built-in guide

Astryx — the “sixth sense · Mind” — reads only your own chart and the Astryx knowledge base, and explains it in plain language.

  • Open it from the ✦ Ask Astryx button in the top bar on almost any screen.
  • Ask anything about your calibration — why a tone, herb, color, or transit appears, or what a term like your Ascendant means. Starter chips get you going.
  • It can set up sessions. Ask about today’s transits, the full-body ladder, or your chakras and a button appears — “Begin today’s calibration →”, “Set up the Full Body ladder →”, “Begin Chakra Recalibration →”. Tapping it opens the Chamber already configured. Astryx never invents a session; it opens the one the engine computed.
  • Listen instead of read — every reply has a 🔊 listen button. Pick her voice in Settings.
Good to know

The free plan includes 20 questions a day (it refreshes tomorrow; your calibration and sessions stay fully available). Astryx is an AI reference companion — not a person, not a professional — and for symptoms or health decisions it will point you to your licensed practitioner. If it ever detects a crisis, it leads with real support resources first.
